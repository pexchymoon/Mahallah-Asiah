    RULES AND REGULATIONS

    DRESS CODE AT MAHALLAH FACILITIES

    Wear hijab until it covers up the chest.

    Sleeves must come to the wrist and make sure the upper dress is long until the bottom area.

    Loosen, no tight pants or fitting jeans.

    Any kind of shoes and sandal except slippers.

    This kind of attire are very important for female even when doing sports
