    FAQ


    Frequenly Asked


    Q: What Is Included In The Cost Of Mahallah?

    A: The Cost Of Mahallah Typically Includes The Cost Of Your Room, As Well As Utilities Such As Electricity, Water, And Maintenance.


    Q: What are the benefits of living in mahallah?

    A: There are many benefits to living in Mahallah, including: Convenience: Living in Mahallah puts you close to your classes, the library, and other university resources. Community: Living in mahallah gives you the opportunity to meet and socialize with other students. Opportunities to get involved: IIUM often host events and activities for Mahallah students, giving you the chance to get involved in University life.


    Q: What are the challenges of living in mahallah?

    A: There are also some challenges to living in college residence, such as: Shared space: If you live in a shared room, you will need to learn to live with other people and respect their space and belongings. Noise: Mahallah can be noisy, especially during late-night study sessions or on weekends.


    Additional FAQ


    Q: Can I pick a roommate?

    A: Yes, mahallah asiah allow you to pick a roommate. You can typically pick a roommate by coming to the mahallah registration at the same time.


    Q: Can I have guests stay with me in mahallah?

    A: guest can only visit for a short time and cannot spend the night in the mahallah.


    Q: What if i want to LIDV?

    A: you have to fill in the form that already been given in your imaalum. go to the mahallah section and you will see LIDV form. fill it and give to the mahallah perspective.


    Q: How to channel my complaint?

    A: Your complaint related to Mahallah maintenance can be channelled through Mahallah Maintenance Reporting System (MMRS)
